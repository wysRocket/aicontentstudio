## Recommended workflow

1. Define campaign objective and KPI.
2. Gather references in Inspiration.
3. Generate copy drafts in Create Post.
4. Build video concepts in Video Generation.
5. Review for brand consistency.
6. Approve and hand off for publishing.

## Review checklist

- Clear audience fit
- Single, explicit CTA
- Channel-appropriate length
- Brand tone consistency
